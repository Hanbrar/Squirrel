## About

Squirrel is a simple, beautiful job application tracker that helps you stay organized during your job search. No sign-up required — just start tracking.

## Features

- **Add Jobs Quickly** — Paste a job URL or enter details manually
- **Track Status** — Bookmarked, Applied, Interview, Offer, Rejected, Ghosted
- **Filter & Search** — Find jobs by status or search by company/title
- **Smart Logos** — Automatically fetches company logos
- **Dark/Light Mode** — Easy on the eyes, day or night
- **Local Storage** — Your data stays in your browser (cookies)
